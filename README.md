# More Vehicles for BTA #

## What is this? ##
This is a third-party submod for the BATTLETECH Advanced Universe (BTAU) mod pack for the game BattleTech (2018) by Harebrained Studios (HBS BT).

It makes some otherwise unplayable vehicles in BTAU now playable (including select Clan vehicles), and adds a number of new vehicles.

## Setup instructions ##
Simply download or check out this Git repository to `BATTLETECH/Mods`, then run the game.

## List of vehicles ##
Most of the new vehicles (not existing in base BTAU) are fielded by their listed originating factions and the various mercenaries.

### Vehicles made playable ###
Inner Sphere vehicles:
- Myrmidon (medium)
- Light TBM5 Carrier (medium)
- Sabaku Kaze (medium)
- Gallant Mk. 3 (heavy)
- Patton Mk. 2 (heavy)
- Brutus (Support) (heavy)
- Heavy MRM Carrier (assault)

Clan vehicles:
- Shamash (light)
- Ares (medium)
- Epona Prime (medium)
- Pike (heavy)
- Morrigu Fire Support Vehicle (assault)

If you want to unlock more vehicles, add the relevant vehicle def IDs to the `TargetIDs` list in `More Vehicles/merge/mergeDef_unlockTanks.json`.

### New variants of canon vehicles ###
- Super Hetzer
	- The boxy assault gun has been redesigned with a Heavy Gauss Rifle.
- Bolla Prototype
	- Pre-Jihad version of the Blakist stealth APC, carrying a Tengu BA squad.
- Merkava EX
    - A demonic upgrade of the original, featuring a Heavy PPC.

### Original vehicles ###
"By Donut Steel Industries"
- Hu Tao
	- A 34-ton Liao update of the Ignis, with four flamethrowers, a Magshot and a Ying Long BA squad.
- Huntress
	- A fast 45-ton Clan tank hunter with six ATM6 launchers.
- Carolean
	- A fast 45-ton tank armed with twin LAC/5s, seeing widespread use due to its low cost.
- Wuguan Siege Gun
	- A 55-ton Liao self-propelled gun with a Long Tom Cannon and 5 tons of ammo (including 1 ton of 'special surprise').
- Desaix
	- A 65-ton Davion high-performance tank, available in three variants with varying configurations.
	
## Shop locations ##
**Inner Sphere-level Civilization planets** will sell the Light TBM5 Carrier, Gallant Mk. 3, Myrmidon, and Carolean if you have some reputation with the planet owner. With higher rep, the Patton Mk. 2, Brutus (Support), and Heavy MRM Carrier become available. The Light TBM5 Carrier and Carolean are also available on **Periphery-level Civilization planets** with higher rep. The **Rasalhague faction store** carries the Carolean.

**Davion planets** sell the Desaix B with some rep. The A and C variants are on **Steiner planets** but require higher rep; these also sell the Super Hetzer with less rep.

**Liao planets** sell the Hu Tao with some rep and Wuguan with high rep.

**Kurita planets** sell the Sabazku Kaze with some rep.

**ComStar planets** sell the Merkava EX with high rep.

The Shamash, Svantovit and Ares are sold on **Clan Diamond Shark** planets, as well as **Clan Nova Cat** planets with some rep. At Honored relations, the Nova Cats will throw in the Epona Prime as well. The Pike and Morrigu can only be obtained from salvage.
